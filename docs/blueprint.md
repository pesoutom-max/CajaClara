# **App Name**: Caja Clara

## Core Features:

- Add Sale: Quickly add a sale with the amount and payment method.
- Record Expense: Log expenses with amount and a brief description, e.g., 'Groceries, supplier, utilities'.
- Daily Snapshot: Display a clear view of the day's sales, expenses, and the expected cash in hand: 'Today's Sales: $___', 'Today's Expenses: $___', 'Expected Cash: $___'.
- Top Products (Optional): Option to add quick products with name and price for faster sales recording. 'What you sell the most'.
- Previous Days: Simple list to view previous days' summaries. 'How your other days went'.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft, warm yellow (#FAD7A0) to inspire ease and clarity.
- Background color: Light yellow (#FAF5EB), almost white, very desaturated and bright, to maintain a sense of simplicity.
- Accent color: Muted orange (#E5A06A) for buttons and important information, providing a gentle contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif to create a blend of modernity and approachability.
- Simple, custom icons that are intuitive and represent daily actions and summaries.
- Clean, single-column layout with high readability and ample spacing to avoid feeling cluttered. Big buttons, short forms.
- Gentle transitions and subtle animations for providing feedback on actions.
